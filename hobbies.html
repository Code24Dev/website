My Hobbies


Baking

I love baking delicious treats like cakes, cookies, and pastries. There's something therapeutic about mixing ingredients and watching them transform in the oven. The sweet aroma of freshly baked goods never fails to lift my spirits.

Baking allows me to express my creativity and share joy with others through homemade goodies. It's a delightful hobby that combines precision and artistry. I love sharing my hobby with others especially with my niece and nephews. Together we make delciious Christmas cookies and Banana Cream Pies.

Playing Guitar

Playing the guitar is my passion. The soothing sound of the strings and the rhythmic melody transport me to a world of musical expression. Whether it's strumming chords or picking intricate melodies, the guitar is my constant companion.

Music has the power to evoke emotions, and playing the guitar allows me to connect with those emotions on a profound level. It's a source of joy, relaxation, and a means of self-expression. I cant say I'm a professional because there is always room to grow. However, I can state that I have a a grasp of a couple of chords and can 'somewhat' play by ear if give a few minutes to find the right chords. I've been playing for about 6 years.

Reading Manga

Immersing myself in captivating stories and vibrant visuals is why I love reading manga. The diverse genres and unique storytelling in manga offer endless entertainment and a window into different worlds. I'm currently reading a mystery manga called Parallel City posted on Webtoons by a writer under the alias 'Goda'.

Exploring the intricate plots and characters keeps me engaged, and the artistic style of manga adds another layer of appreciation. It's a hobby that sparks my imagination and provides a relaxing pastime.
